About Us

Meet Zappy and his buddies who have made this possible

Our Mission

ZaapCodes is dedicated to making building codes accessible, fast, and easy to understand for developers, architects, contractors, and the general public. We zap you the code info you need – instantly!

What We Do

We combine data scraping, geolocation, and intuitive UI to help users find the relevant construction codes for any address in Georgia. Our mission is to take the confusion out of code compliance.

Our Team

We’re a small team of engineers and designers passionate about civic tech, building tools that help communities navigate bureaucracy with ease.
